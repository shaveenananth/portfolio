Voice-to-Text Notepad

"This is a simple Python program that listens to your voice and converts it into text. It saves the text in groups of five words at a time. You can speak naturally, and the program will transcribe your words. It will stop listening when you say 'bye,' 'stop,' or 'exit.' This basic voice recognition project uses simple commands to provide an easy way to transcribe spoken words into written text.
